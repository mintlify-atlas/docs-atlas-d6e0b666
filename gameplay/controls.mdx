---
title: "Controls"
description: "How to interact with Memorama - Forsaken Casino on desktop, mobile, and VR."
---

The game runs in a WebXR-capable A-Frame scene. All interaction is gaze-based — there is no traditional click mechanic.

<Note>
  You do not need to click on a card to flip it. Moving the red cursor over a card triggers the `mouseenter` event, which flips the card automatically.
</Note>

## The red cursor

A red cursor is always visible at the center of your screen. It is an A-Frame cursor attached to the camera with a `raycaster` that targets all `.clickable` elements (the cards). Wherever you look, the cursor follows. Hover it over a face-down card to flip it.

## Camera starting position

The camera rig starts at position `0 1.6 1.2` with a downward rotation of `-25°` on the X-axis. This angles your view directly toward the card table — no manual adjustment is needed before you start playing.

## Controls by platform

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop">
    | Action | Control |
    |---|---|
    | Look around | Mouse drag or trackpad drag (`look-controls`) |
    | Move through the scene | **W** / **A** / **S** / **D** keys (`wasd-controls`, acceleration: 10) |
    | Flip a card | Move the red cursor over a face-down card (hover only — no click) |

    On desktop, `look-controls` captures pointer input and rotates the camera. Use WASD to reposition yourself if needed, though the default camera position already faces the table.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile">
    A-Frame's `look-controls` supports touch-based look controls in mobile browsers. Drag your finger across the screen to rotate your view.

    Moving through the scene with WASD is not available on mobile. To flip a card, rotate your view so the red cursor lands on a face-down card.

    <Note>
      Performance and experience on mobile may vary depending on the device and browser. A desktop browser or VR headset is recommended for the best experience.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="VR headset">
    Memorama uses A-Frame 1.4.0, which supports WebXR natively. Open the game in a WebXR-compatible browser on your headset and enter VR mode.

    Interaction is gaze-based: look at a face-down card to flip it. The red cursor follows your gaze and triggers the `mouseenter` event when it lands on a `.clickable` card.

    No controller input is required.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

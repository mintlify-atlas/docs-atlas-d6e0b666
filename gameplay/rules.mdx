## Objective

Find all **4 matching pairs** among the 8 cards on the table before the 40-second **VIDA** timer reaches zero. Match every pair and you escape. Run out of time and you lose your soul.

## Card layout

The board contains 8 cards arranged in a **4√ó2 grid** (4 columns, 2 rows). Cards are shuffled randomly at the start of every round using `[...cardData].sort(() => Math.random() - 0.5)`, so the layout changes each game. There are exactly 4 unique image pairs: `img1`, `img2`, `img3`, `img4`.

## Turn structure

1. Hover the red cursor over a face-down card ‚Äî it flips face-up.
2. Hover the red cursor over a second face-down card ‚Äî it flips face-up.
3. The game evaluates the pair:
   - **Match** ‚Äî both cards disappear from the board after 600ms.
   - **No match** ‚Äî both cards flip back face-down after 800ms, and you try again.

## Win and lose conditions

| Outcome | Trigger | Message displayed | What happens next |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Win** | All 4 pairs found (`pairsFound === 4`) | SALISTE CON VIDA | Page reloads automatically after 4 seconds |
| **Lose** | Timer reaches 0 | PERDISTE TU ALMA | Red overlay fades in, page reloads after 3 seconds |

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens when two cards are face-up and being evaluated?">
    While two cards are being compared, the board is locked (`lockBoard = true`). Hovering over any other card has no effect until the outcome animation completes and the lock is released. This prevents accidental or deliberate multi-flips.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I hover over a card that has already been matched?">
    No. Matched cards are removed from the board entirely ‚Äî they shrink to scale `0 0 0` and are no longer in play.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I hover over a card that is already face-up?">
    Hovering over a card whose `dataset.flipped` is `"true"` does nothing. Only face-down cards respond to the cursor.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When does the timer start?">
    The 40-second timer starts on your **first card hover** ‚Äî not when the page loads. You can study the board before you commit to flipping a card.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when the timer hits zero?">
    The `die()` function is called. The message **PERDISTE TU ALMA** appears, a dark red overlay fades in over 1 second to cover your view, and the page reloads automatically after 3 seconds.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does the game restart automatically?">
    Yes. Whether you win or lose, the game calls `location.reload()` to restart. You do not need to refresh the page manually.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are cards reshuffled each round?">
    Yes. Every time the page loads, the card data array is shuffled with `[...cardData].sort(() => Math.random() - 0.5)`, so the positions of the pairs change each round.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What is Memorama?

Memorama is the Spanish name for the classic concentration / memory card game. Players flip cards face-up two at a time, trying to find matching pairs. All unmatched cards are flipped back face-down after a short delay. The goal is to find all pairs before time runs out.

**Forsaken Casino** wraps this familiar mechanic in a fully 3D WebVR environment rendered via [A-Frame](https://aframe.io), playable directly in any modern browser — no installation required.
